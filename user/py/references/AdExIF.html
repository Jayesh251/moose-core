AdExIF¶

class AdExIF¶

Leaky Integrate-and-Fire neuron with Exponential spike rise and adaptation via an adapting current w.Rm*Cm * dVm/dt = -(Vm-Em) + deltaThresh * exp((Vm-thresh)/deltaThresh) + Rm*I - w tau_w * d w /dt = a0*(Vm-Em) - w at each spike, w -> w + b0

w¶

double (value field) adaptation current with time constant tauW

tauW¶

double (value field) time constant of adaptation current w

a0¶

double (value field) factor for voltage-dependent term in evolution of adaptation current: tau_w dw/dt = a0*(Vm-Em) - w
